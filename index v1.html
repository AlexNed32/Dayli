How it works

1

Plan

Split your 24 hours a day on tasks e.g.: Sleeping, Work, Family Time, Hobby, etc. and assign the proper time for each
2

Track

Track every movement during the day and assign it to the right category
3

Evalute

Study what you spend your time on. Fill your free time with important things and avoid putting them off
